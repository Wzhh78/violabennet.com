Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

We are here to answer your questions about the Viola Bennet brand, products, and collaborations.

Brand & Story

Is Viola Bennet a brand, a person, or both?

Viola Bennet is both a brand and the name of the novel's protagonist, who is also the core jewelry artist. It represents a multidisciplinary art concept spanning music, literature, and jewelry design.

What is the core philosophy of the Viola Bennet brand?

Our core philosophy is "Wearable Poetry." We are dedicated to transforming deep emotions and artistic inspiration into tangible jewelry pieces, moving music, and touching novels.

Jewelry Products

What materials are used in Viola Bennet jewelry?

Our jewelry primarily uses **natural freshwater cultured pearls**, **925 sterling silver**, and various **natural gemstones**. We commit that all materials comply with ethical sourcing standards, with specific details provided in the product descriptions.

How should I care for my Viola Bennet jewelry?

Please avoid exposing jewelry to chemicals (like perfumes or cleaners). Store pieces in a dry, sealed jewelry box when not wearing them. Regularly polish gently with a soft cloth to maintain their luster.

Where can I purchase Viola Bennet jewelry?

Our jewelry is currently sold mainly through our official Amazon store (link available on the homepage) and via our occasional online pop-up stores.

Order & Shipping

Do you offer worldwide shipping?

Yes, we offer worldwide shipping through the Amazon platform or designated logistics providers. Specific shipping costs and times depend on your delivery address; please check the details during checkout.

How do I process a return or exchange?

All returns and exchanges follow the policy of the channel through which you made the purchase (such as Amazon). Please contact the customer service of the original platform or reach out to us via our contact email for assistance.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Answers to your questions about our jewelry, novels, and brand.

Where can I learn more about Viola Bennet and her creative vision?

Viola Bennet is a brand encompassing jewelry, love novels, and original music. You can find more details about her journey, inspiration, and philosophy on the Brand Story page.

What materials are used in Viola Bennet jewelry?

Our jewelry pieces are made using high-quality, ethically sourced materials, primarily including S925 sterling silver and high-grade alloys, often plated with precious metals like 14k or 18k gold. We use AAA-grade cubic zirconia (CZ) or semi-precious stones for sparkle.

Where can I buy Viola Bennet's novels?

Viola Bennet's love novels are available through major international online retailers, including Amazon worldwide. You can find direct links on our Home Page (Novels section) or by searching for "Viola Bennet" on Amazon.

Where can I listen to Viola Bennet's original music?

Her original music, often the official soundtracks for her novels, is primarily hosted on her official YouTube channel. You can access it directly via the Music link in our navigation bar.

What are the shipping costs and delivery times?

Since Viola Bennet jewelry is currently sold primarily through platforms like Amazon, shipping costs and delivery times are governed by the specific policies of that platform in your region. Please refer to your cart and checkout page on Amazon for accurate, up-to-date information.

What is your policy on returns and exchanges?

All returns and exchanges follow the policy of the channel through which you made the purchase (such as Amazon). Please contact the customer service of the original platform or reach out to us via our contact email for assistance.
